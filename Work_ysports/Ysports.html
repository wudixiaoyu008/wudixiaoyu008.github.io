1. CONTEXT

Design Background

Yahoo Sports is one of the fans most favorite sports apps in North America. It keeps evolving to make it easy to connect teams, players, and games, so that sports fans never miss a moment. In 2018, it started to provide the NFL live streaming service to fans, aiming to increase the mobile app DAU. With the big investment and usage surge in related modules, the overall retention and engagement are still fairly low.

My Role

My goal is to drive mobile app retention and engagement. I uncovered the problems, led the design strategy and the design for mobile growth, including user onboarding, scores tab, big sports coverage. Also built the design system with 2 other designers.

4. User Onboarding

What Users Like In Onboarding?

So, Those Ideas Were Killed

“What matters is not growth of users, it's growth of users completing the core action.“

What Is The Core Action?

The action that is the very foundation and essence of your product. Pinterest would not exist without pinning. Twitter would not exist without tweeting. What’s YouTube if people didn’t upload videos? Facebook wouldn’t exist if users weren’t friending. For Yahoo Sports, the core action is adding favorite teams.

What’s The Best Way?

I made prototypes in Framer, and tested through in-lab user studies, also fans night. Option 3 wins. Because it's more efficient, users can navigate to different leagues from left sidebar, and scroll less than the view pager. Users also prefer the bigger and exciting logos compared with accordion. At last, I combined the header and search bar to save vertical space.

Drive Users Come Back

Besides letting users complete the core actions, we also need a scheme to drive users come back, which is the notification. I synced with Yahoo Mail Growth PMs, to get their insights, including the order, copy, preferred formats, etc.

Most importantly, adding a pre-prompt step is always better. And always keep in mind fans like the fandom (favorite teams), and dislike too many notifications.

Overall Onboarding Flow

We decided to remove the step of adding favorite leagues, because we can add them for users automatically once they select the favorite teams.

We also moved the notification permission step from beginning to the end. During a/b testing, these two perform at the same level. We aligned to put it in the end, because it conforms more to users mental model since they already input a lot effort.

Wait A Minute!

There is a loading time, and it's usually around 7 seconds. Should we grab this opportunity to utilize the 7 seconds? I designed a welcome video and inserted to the loading time, delivering the value props and killer features to users. We can also customize it in different sports seasons.

7. Mobile Reskin

Utilize The New Tool

We used to rely on Google Drive File Stream to collaborate. But when it comes to the design system, it can no longer support the tasks. We fast integrated Abstract, utilize the designers' git for version control and collaboration.

Design System

Modern Reskin

We reskined the entire product, including both iOS and Android, leveraging the new design system. Aiming to provide a more consistent and delightful experience.

User Quotes

We tested wildly the new design through in-lab testing, fans night, and in-app survey. The reskin got lots of positive feedback. After launch, our NPS surged mainly due to the reskin.
