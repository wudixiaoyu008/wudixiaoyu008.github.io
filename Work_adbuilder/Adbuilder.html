CREATING ADS HAS NEVER BEEN THIS EASY


As the only designer, defined and designed the entire incubated product, migrated to VM Native ad platform.

The conversion rate is 284% compared to the Native ad creation.

1. CONTEXT

Design Background

Currently Verizon Media has two ad platforms, DSP and Native. Those platforms are mainly for big advertisers and agencies. We wanna create a new advertising product that allows non-advertisers (such as small business owners without previous advertising experience) to promote their business on Verizon Media’s properties such as Yahoo, HuffPost, and TechCrunch.

My Role

This's my first project after I rotated to ads team. As the only designer, I defined and redesigned the entire product. led the ideation, design strategy and the design execution for web and mobile web. It's a small team, besides me, there are only one PM and three engineers.

Why We Build It?

2. Proof Of Concept

The Challenges

Limited Resource

No team, no time, no budget. The leadership strategy is not on SMB. Our team is like an experiment, trying to prove the SMB value within 3 months. And we have very little budget.

Competitive Market

Google, Facebook, Snapchat... Just name a few. The self-serve advertising marketing for SMB is really competitive. They all have been really successful with a large user base.



Vision & Goals

Imagine a world where non-advertisers can create an appealing ad in less than 2 minutes.

We start our journey with above challenges, also with the ambitious vision. As a product designer, it is my responsibility to streamline the ad creation experience for our users and help them reach the right customers with the most relevant information.

Start With MVP


We decided to cut all unnecessary features in Native, only keep the ad creation flow. The MVP version allows users to target customers based on their location, demography, interests, etc. They can create a standard native ad simply by providing audience info, an image, and contact & payment info. Design Strategy

The MVP works! The remained key steps are exactly what users want. But there are lots of problems, like broken UX issues that prevent users move forward. Now it's time to input more design to fix the issues and help it grow.

We decided to have 2 phases. First, we need polish the ad creation. That's the only function we have, and the drop off rate is 87%. If we don't fix that, even though we acquire more users, we are gonna lose them. Then, we need think through opportunities to drive the overall growth, like acquisition, activation, and retention.

3. Fix The Conversion

User Research Findings

Who Are Our Users
Research Findings
Some users didn’t feel confident and trust in the system. They were hesitant to proceed with account creation or payment setup.

Solution Overview

How I Get There

Typical design process is helpful when solving unknown and vague problems. This time, our goal is clear, which is optimizing the conversion. So we leverage a new process, we track the data funnel, find opportunities, test based on the hypothesis and iterate from the learnings.

How To Create An Ad

The redesigned ad creation page is more simple and intuitive. Conversion rate jumped from 54.55% to 83.33%. We only keep one image uploader and one ad preview. Users don't feel overwhelmed anymore. Also explicit the action that users can search images from our sponsored site. So users won't quit even they don't have images at hand.

Set Up The Budget

We used to provide users a default budget with another two options. We assume this would remove the user friction, since they just need click next to move forward. But during user testing, we figured out they actually don't trust it, they think it's just our trick. They'd prefer to input manually with more info to make the decision. So we provide the slider, also forecast the competitors range. It led the conversion rate increased from 70.83% to 82.05%.

Forecasting Module

I redesigned the forecasting module, it will be updated along the ad creation journey. To avoid user frustration caused by the low forecasting number for small geo, we hide the number and showing the alert message, suggesting users to expand the targeting.

Funnel Comparison

Overall the conversion rate jumped from 13.84% to 43.38%. The biggest jump is the last step. We used to use Yahoo membership login. That didn't work well. Since the flow itself is complicated, also the inconsistency branding makes users confused. After changed to the IDB2B simpler sign up, that removed lots of the friction.

4. Drive Overall Growth

With Limited Resources

After fixed the ad creation conversion rate. We moved on the 2nd phase, drive the overall growth. At this stage, having very little budget is our main challenge. We don't have much money to source and acquire new users, also don't have enough budget to encourage them to spend money and stay.

Drive The Acquisition

We have to acquire new users mainly from internal resources and collaboration. We promoted Ad Builder on Yahoo Search, media properties. Added entry points from Native ad platform and Yahoo Small Business website. Also created a new direct in Yahoo homepage. Users can choose Advertise with us, and will be landed to Ad Builder, this covers 30% of our user acquisition.

Lift The Activation

Once users are landed on Ad Builder, we wanna convert them right away as many as possible. I designed and coded the landing page, highlight our uniqueness, your ads will only run on our premium inventory. Also provided coupon credit and import from Facebook features, to encourage users try out and make switch from competitors smoothly. I also designed & coded other widgets (contact us, FAQ and blog) to make the process be more self-serving, due to the limited customer support resource.

Improve The Retention

Advertising is not an one time deal, we hope users can retain and keep spending. The most effective way is to drive them come back and look at the dashboard, showing the ad pacing and metrics. I designed a series of automatic email templates and the triggering scheme, also build the redesigned the dashboard based on the Verizon Media guideline.

Final Outcome

Within the 3 months, we cold started and keep iterating, finally broke through the 3,500 account created, advertiser spend is more than $150k, and the 30 day retention is 70%.

5. Migrated To Native

“ The flow is so simple and better than Facebook, but I just think Facebook can bring me more customers. ”

Wait A Minute!

There is a thing called human organ transplant. You can still contribute or be alive even you are dead. We have tried many innovations on Ad Builder, what if we can leverage the learnings to other platforms?

Transplanted To Native

We transplanted it to Native. These two share the same backend. We wanna see whether Ad Builder ad creation funnel is helpful to create Native's conversion rate. On Native landing page, when users click Create ad now button. 50% of users are landed to Ad Builder funnel, the other 50% go through the original Native funnel. Ad Builder wins, 287% compared with the Native flow. My baby is still alive, living in Native platform.
