INTERNATIONAL TRAVEL CAN BE EASY AND SMOOTH


Redesign China Sourthern Airlines app, help the client fulfill international travelers' needs. Launched Mar. 2017.



1. CONTEXT

Design Background

China Southern Airlines is the largest airline in Asia and the fourth largest in the world. Its mobile app was outdated and could no longer meet modern requirements, especially for international travelers. China Southern Airlines reached out to our team to redesigned the global app as freelancer.

My Role

I led the design strategy, core flows UX, and the design system across iOS and Android. Helped marketing research, user research, and conceptual proposals with 4 other designers.

Design Process

2. RESEARCH

Current Pain Points

Before jumping into design, we did a heuristic evaluation on the current app. Figure out the unmet needs and pain points that can be improved.

Low efficiency

The hamburger menu hides some of the main functions.

Consistency issue

Similar elements are not consistent across different pages.

Missing edge case

There is no place to book multi-city flights.

International unfriendly

The input format of mobile number is not globally welcoming.

Negative connotation

In Asian culture, the downward facing plane has a negative connotation.

Information disorder

Time should be more apparent than the reference number.

Market Research

China Southern Airlines didn't have a global app at that time, so we chose FlyDelta to assess the market. FlyDelta helped us understand the current user journey, especially the ticket booking, online check-in, electronic boarding pass, and the loyalty incentive that comes with the app.

Large Scale User Survey

We conducted preliminary user sessions for 45 customers before sending out a fully-scaled survey to a wide breadth of CS Airlines users (more than 1k).

3. DESIGN OUTCOME

Design Strategy

Focus on the MVP

Due to the limited timeline and engineering resource, the client scoped down the requirements: let's only focus on the core user flows. We landed on ticket purchase, and trip management flows after rounds of sketches.

Deliverable Overview

We agreed to deliver the end to end core user flows across iOS and Android, also with the visual guideline and conceptual ideas.

4. FEATURE GO THROUGH

Easy Booking Steps

An overwhelming booking flow was one of the biggest problems in the old design. For the booking redesign, we introduced the divide and conquer philosophy, "One screen, one task".

Smooth Check-in

Smooth check-in is essential for busy travelers, especially for international travelers. We cut out unnecessary steps and provided a more intuitive check-in experience.

Visualize Seat Map

Interacting with the seat map a mobile device can be frustrating. We researched different types of airplanes and visualized seat maps for common Boeing and Air Bus airplanes with readability and accessibility.

Entertainment

Travel is more than just getting from point A to point B, it can be a truly enjoyable process. With the focus on elevating the travel experience for our users, we proposed some conceptual features for clients.

Say Something

Airport is a place that may trigger emotions and bring back memories. Let it out— share them anonymously with people nearby, maybe you will even find your soulmate.

Airport Camera

Waiting is tedious, how about letting your friends know your exciting adventure ahead by starting from a fun selfie with all the cute customizable bizarre add-ons.

Terminal Map

Let this function help you navigate through the terminal. Grab a bite at a featured restaurant or pick up a last-minute gift at a duty-free shop, fun discovery only takes a fingertip to do so.
